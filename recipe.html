Pizza Recipe

ingredients directions
1 1/2 cups warm water (105°F-115°F) Make and knead the pizza dough
1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) of active dry yeast Proof the yeast
3 1/2 cups bread flour 2 Tbsp olive oil
2 teaspoons salt
Olive oil Cornmeal(to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
Tomato sauce Mozzarella cheese, grated
1 teaspoon sugar Make and knead the pizza dough
Put dough in warm place to rise
Preheat pizza stone Prep toppings Flatten dough ball, and stretch out into a round Brush dough top with olive oil Sprinkle pizza peel with corn meal, put flattened dough on top Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings Sprinkle cornmeal on pizza stone, slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven Bake pizza(10-15 mins)
